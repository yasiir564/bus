Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ticket?
You can book a ticket easily through our online booking form. Select your route, date, and number of passengers, then click 'Book Now'.

We offer fast and reliable parcel delivery services between Garissa and Nairobi. You can send parcels of various sizes through our parcel service.
Are your buses comfortable?
We offer both Standard and VIP coaches with comfortable seating, air conditioning, and additional amenities like WiFi in our VIP coaches.
